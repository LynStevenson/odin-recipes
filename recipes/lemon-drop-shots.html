Lemon Drop Shots

Description

Make them. Drink them. Enjoy them. Party with them. Lemon Drops. (tm)

Ingredients

  • ¾ fluid ounce vodka (such as Absolut®)
  • ¾ fluid ounce lemon juice
  • 2 ½ teaspoons white sugar, divided, or more to taste
  • 1 lemon, cut into wedges

Steps

  1. Combine vodka and lemon juice in a shot glass. Sprinkle 1/2 teaspoon sugar on top.
  2. Place remaining 2 teaspoons sugar on a small plate; dip lemon wedges in sugar.
    Suck on a sugared lemon after drinking the shot.
